How it began

I used to work long shifts on construction sites loud, tiring, and often lonely. Books opened a quiet space for me. A small group of us started reading together; those pages became a place to breathe and be seen. From that simple circle, ReadersConnect grew into a private community of readers who care about stories and the people who write them.

We’re not a marketing agency or a paid review group. We are readers everyday people who read closely and leave honest feedback that helps good books find real readers.

How it works

When an author invites us, they choose a reader size. We run gentle, focused reading cycles usually 7, 14, or 21 days. Readers read together, share impressions, and leave policy-compliant reviews on Amazon and Goodreads.

Share

Authors submit a digital copy and choose how many readers they'd like (30–200+).

Read

Readers join a short, supported cycle and discuss the book in private threads.

Review

Vetted readers post honest, policy-compliant reviews that reflect their real experience.
