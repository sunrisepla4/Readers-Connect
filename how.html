From submission to honest review — a clear, careful process

Our reading circles are designed to respect the book and the author. Each step is intentional and transparent so authors feel safe and readers feel valued.

1

Submit your book

Authors provide a digital copy and choose a reader group size (e.g., 30, 50, 200). You may indicate preferences for genre or reader experience.

2

Verification & selection

We verify the book and check for availability, platform compliance, and any sensitive content. This step helps protect authors and readers from misunderstandings.

3

We match readers

We select a group of vetted readers matched by genre, background, and availability everyday people who read for the craft and community.

4

Reading cycle

Readers enter a short, supported challenge (7, 14, or 21 days). They read together, discuss key moments in private threads, and reflect on the book’s craft and voice.

5

Community discussion

Readers share impressions, favorite passages, and polite critiques all aimed at helping authors understand how readers experience the book.

6

Honest reviews

Vetted readers post thoughtful, policy-compliant reviews on Amazon and Goodreads. These are real reactions from real people no scripts, no paid farms.

A few things authors often ask

1. Who are the Readers in ReadersConnect? We are not a marketing agency or paid review group. Our readers are everyday people teachers, artists, musicians, parents, students, and professionals who genuinely love stories. Every review comes from someone who has actually read your book and cares about giving honest, thoughtful feedback.

2. How does the reading process work? When an author shares their digital book with us, they choose how many readers they want to participate (30–50 for smaller circles or 200+ for larger cycles). We then run 7-day, 14-day, or 21-day reading challenges, depending on book length. During this time, readers dive into your story, discuss it in private groups, and leave authentic reviews on Amazon.

3. Are reviews real and honest? Absolutely. We never script or exaggerate reviews. Our readers share their genuine reactions while following Amazon’s review policies. The goal is to provide meaningful, organic feedback while helping your book gain visibility with real readers.

4. Why is there a small “thank-you gesture” for readers? Readers have jobs, families, and busy lives. A small gesture like a coffee or snack from the author keeps them motivated and engaged, but it is not a payment for reviews. It’s simply a token of appreciation that makes the experience warm and personal.

5. Is my book safe with ReadersConnect? Yes. Our community is invite-only and private. Your book will only be seen by trusted members, and we take every precaution to maintain privacy and security. No spam, no public exposure just a safe, caring environment.

6. Can I participate multiple times? Of course. Many authors come back with new titles or new editions. Each book cycle is a fresh opportunity to connect with engaged readers and receive authentic feedback.

7. How do I join or share my book? Currently, our community is invite-only. To share your book, simply fill out our contact or invite request form. We review all requests personally to ensure a trusted, respectful experience for both authors and readers.

8. How fast will I get feedback? Reading challenges are designed to fit real readers’ schedules. Typically, authors receive reviews within 1–3 weeks after the cycle ends. We personally monitor each challenge to ensure readers are engaged and timely feedback is delivered.

9. Can I communicate with the readers? Direct communication is limited to maintain privacy and comfort for readers. However, you can share notes, thank-you gestures, or book updates through the challenge organizer (founder). We act as the bridge between authors and readers to maintain trust.

10. Why should I choose ReadersConnect over other services? Because we are human-first, not business-first. No algorithms, no paid reviews, no fake hype. Just real readers who love books and want to support authors genuinely. Your book matters, and our mission is to help it reach readers who appreciate it most.

Reading Challenges

7-Day Challenge

Ideal for short books or readers with more availability. Readers take 7 days to finish the book, then post their honest review at the end.

14-Day Challenge

Suitable for medium-length books or readers with moderate availability. Readers use 14 days to immerse themselves, then leave their review.

21-Day Challenge

For longer books or readers with limited time. They have 21 days to read fully, then provide thoughtful, honest feedback at the end of the cycle.

How a Reading Cycle Works

Step 1: Book Assigned

The author shares their digital book with the ReadersConnect community. We confirm the number of readers and start the cycle based on book length and reader availability.

Step 2: Assigned Reading Period

Readers get 7, 14, or 21 days to read the book depending on its length and their schedule. During this period, they immerse themselves in the story without sharing reviews yet.

Step 3: Reviews Posted

After the reading period ends, readers share their honest, thoughtful reviews on Amazon. All feedback is genuine, heartfelt, and policy-compliant.
